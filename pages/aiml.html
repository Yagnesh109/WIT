Dr. Priya Kulkarni Head of Department

It gives me great pleasure to introduce the Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning department at Walchand Institute of Technology, offering strong undergraduate and postgraduate programmes with a clear focus on intelligence, automation, and data science.

The AIML programme prepares students for careers in machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, robotics, and ethical AI. Our curriculum blends foundational AI theory with applied lab work, industry projects, and research collaboration.

Students benefit from modern AI labs, access to cloud AI platforms, and industry partnerships that support internships, research projects, and applied innovation.

Our vision is to develop AI professionals who can build intelligent systems, solve real-world challenges, and contribute to socially responsible, sustainable technology solutions.

Course Outcomes

After completing each course, students will be able to:

  • Apply mathematics, computing, and AI principles to solve real-world problems.
  • Use modern tools, platforms, and frameworks for data analysis and model development.
  • Develop effective communication, teamwork, and project management skills.
  • Build readiness for advanced AI and machine learning topics through strong practical learning.

Program Outcomes

Engineering graduates will be able to demonstrate:

  1. Engineering knowledge: Apply mathematics, science, and AI concepts to solve complex problems.
  2. Problem analysis: Identify, formulate, and analyze problems using data and models.
  3. Design/development: Design intelligent systems that meet societal needs with ethical considerations.
  4. Investigations: Use research-based knowledge and data-driven methods to reach valid conclusions.
  5. Modern tool usage: Select and apply appropriate AI tools, programming languages, and platforms.
  6. Engineer and society: Assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural impacts of AI systems.
  7. Sustainability: Understand the impact of intelligent systems on sustainable development.
  8. Ethics: Apply ethical principles and professional responsibilities in AI development.
  9. Teamwork: Function effectively in multidisciplinary AI and data science teams.
  10. Communication: Communicate effectively through technical reports, presentations, and documentation.
  11. Project management: Apply engineering and project management principles in intelligent system development.
  12. Life-long learning: Recognize the need for independent and continuing education.

Program Specific Outcomes

  1. Apply AI and ML methods to analyze data, build models, and engineer intelligent solutions.
  2. Design AI systems for real-world applications with robustness, scalability, and user focus.
  3. Use modern tools, frameworks, and cloud platforms to develop and deploy intelligent applications.

Program Educational Objectives

Graduates will:

  1. Exhibit strong foundational knowledge and technical skills in AI, data science, and machine learning.
  2. Demonstrate the ability to solve complex problems and innovate with intelligent technologies.
  3. Remain sensitive to ethical, societal, and environmental responsibilities while pursuing lifelong learning.
